Our Minta System

Minta is our 200 mm product showcasing our patterned deposition technology.

Features
- 200 nm to 50 um lateral patterning resolution
- Up to 8" (200 mm) wafer processing
- 50-400 °C processing temperature
- ALD and CVD precursors compatible
